Business Intelligence

💡 Key Business Insights

12 actionable findings derived from the analysis.

🏆
01

West Region Leads in Sales

West region contributes the highest revenue. Marketing budget should be prioritised here for maximum ROI.

📦
02

Laptops Drive Most Revenue

Laptop category generates the highest sales volume. Ensure adequate stock ahead of peak months to avoid stockouts.

💎
03

Accessories = Hidden Profit

Accessories have the highest profit margin (40%+) despite low sales value. Cross-sell at every device purchase.

📅
04

November is the Best Month

Sales spike in Q4 / November. Plan inventory build-up in October and launch campaigns 2 weeks before peak.

📉
05

March Has Lowest Sales

March is consistently the weakest month. Run clearance offers and bundle deals to stimulate demand.

👤
06

Top 10 = 25% of Revenue

A small group of customers drives a large share of sales. VIP loyalty programmes can protect this revenue stream.

🌍
07

East Has Best Profit Margin

Despite lower sales, East region shows the highest profit margin. Investigate and replicate their cost model.

🛒
08

AOV Can Be Increased

Average Order Value of ₹46,974 can be pushed 15-20% higher through bundle deals and upselling strategies.

📆
09

Weekends Outperform Weekdays

Weekend sales are significantly higher. Increase floor staff and run weekend-exclusive flash sales to capitalise.

⚠️
10

Some Products Lose Money

A few products show negative cumulative profit. Review pricing or discontinue to protect overall margins.

📊
11

Q4 Dominates Quarterly Sales

Q4 contributes ~30% of annual revenue. Align supply chain, logistics, and staffing to handle the volume.

🔗
12

Sales & Profit Are Correlated

Strong positive correlation between sales and profit shows healthy pricing discipline across most product lines.
